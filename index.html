Hydronic Systems

SWS Mechanical specializes in the design, installation, and maintenance of advanced hydronic systems, offering highly efficient and comfortable heating and cooling solutions for a wide range of commercial and multifamily applications. These systems leverage the power of water or other fluids circulated through a network of pipes to precisely control indoor temperatures.

From radiant floor heating to chilled beams and snow melt systems, hydronic technology provides superior thermal comfort and significant energy savings compared to traditional HVAC methods. Our expertise ensures that your hydronic system is custom-designed for optimal performance, integrating electronic controls and smart sensors for efficient energy utilization and responsive temperature regulation.

We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, reliable hydronic solutions that enhance safety, reduce operational costs, and minimize environmental impact for properties across Utah.

Our Hydronic System Services Include:

Custom Hydronic System Design

Tailored designs for radiant heating, cooling, and integrated climate control.

Efficient Installation & Integration

Expert installation ensuring seamless integration and optimal energy performance.

System Optimization & Controls

Advanced electronic controls and sensors for precise temperature management and energy efficiency.

Scheduled Maintenance & Support

Proactive maintenance programs to ensure long-term reliability and peak efficiency.

Snow Melt Systems

In regions with challenging winter weather, maintaining clear and safe walkways, driveways, and critical access points is paramount. SWS Mechanical specializes in the design, installation, and maintenance of automated snow melt systems, ensuring your commercial and multifamily properties remain accessible and secure during the coldest months.

Our snow melt systems effectively prevent the accumulation of snow and ice, significantly reducing the risk of slips and falls, and eliminating the need for manual snow removal. By circulating warm fluid through durable PEX tubing buried beneath surfaces, these systems offer unparalleled convenience, cost savings, and environmental benefits.

We provide customized system designs, precise installations, and regular maintenance to guarantee long-term performance, energy efficiency, and adherence to all industry standards and local regulations. Trust SWS Mechanical to keep your property clear and safe, even in the harshest winter conditions.

Benefits of SWS Snow Melt Systems:

Enhanced Safety

Eliminates hazardous ice and snow, reducing slip-and-fall risks for occupants and visitors.

Automated Convenience

Provides automated snow removal, freeing up staff and resources from manual clearing.

Cost Savings

Reduces labor costs, equipment wear, and potential property damage from plowing and de-icing chemicals.

Property Preservation

Protects concrete, asphalt, and landscaping from corrosive salts and physical damage.
